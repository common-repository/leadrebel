LeadRebel identifies visitor of your website and generates B2B Leads for your company. With help of this plugin, you can install LeadRebel tracking code on your website.

== Description ==

LeadRebel is a software for B2B lead generation. Out plugin shows you which companies visited your website, company and contact information of the contact person. More traffic, more “warm” leads, more sales success! See who visits your website and generate more B2B leads.

How it works: install the plugin, sign up or login (if you have an account) using your plugin. The tracking code will be automatically added to your website.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is LeadRebel®? =
LeadRebel® is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) tool that shows you which company-visitors have visited your site providing information about them. For each visitor, you can see the company name, address, telephone number, email, company description, the company category and size, and a list of employees. It's a software that analyses your web traffic, supports your sales team, and provides you with "warm" leads.

= How can I use LeadRebel®? =
To use LeadRebel®, you must install and activate the plugin. If you already have an account, click on login, input your LeadRebel-credentials and your website will be automatically connected with the plugin. If you don’t have a LeadRebel account yet, simply signup using the plugin or sign up on the website: https://leadrebel.io/signup

= How can I follow up on LeadRebel® data? =
Read this guideline and learn how you can integrate LeadRebel® into your marketing and sales: https://blog.leadrebel.io/how-to-use-data-from-leadrebel

= How much does LeadRebel® cost? =
You can use the full version of our website for 14 days free of charge. Premium version price: 29 Euro per month (Lite), 89 Euro per month (Standard), and 299 Euro per month (Premium).

= How do I pay for LeadRebel®? =
You can pay by credit card and PayPal. More payment features will be integrated soon.

= Where does the company data come from? =
Company data comes from open sources and corporate websites.

= Where do the contact details of the employees come from? =
The employee data displayed on LeadRebel® also come from open sources and can easily be found by searching the Internet.

= How does LeadRebel® recognize which companies visited my website? =
Our IP Tracker analyses the IP of the visitor and determines which company the IP belongs to.

= Can I export leads? =
Yes, you can export leads as Excel data, PDF file, or mail. We are working on the integration of various CRMs and email services, and soon you will be able to export the leads in this way.

= Are LeadRebel® GDPR compliant? =
Yes, our software is GDPR compliant. Please note our privacy policy: https://leadrebel.io/privacy. We respect your data and privacy and make every effort to follow all GDPR guidelines.

= Is my data safe? =
Your data is encrypted in our database. Only a very restricted circle of employees has access to it. We make every effort to improve our security standards to the best of our knowledge and belief so that no unauthorized person gains access to the data.


== Installation ==

1. Go to `Plugins` in the Admin menu
2. Click on the button `Add new`
3. Search for `Leadrebel` and click 'Install Now' or click on the `upload` link to upload `leadrebel.zip`
4. Click on `Activate plugin`
